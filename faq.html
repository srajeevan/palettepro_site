Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this for digital art or physical painting?

A: Physical painting! While digital artists can use it, Palette Pro is specifically designed to help oil, acrylic, and gouache painters mix real paint matches.

Q: Do I need specific paint brands?

A: No. We use universal pigment names (like "Burnt Sienna" or "Ultramarine Blue") found in almost every professional paint line (Winsor & Newton, Gamblin, Golden, etc.).

Q: Can I use it offline?

A: Yes! Most features like the Value Map and Squint Mode work completely offline. Cloud syncing requires an internet connection.

Q: How do I delete my account?

A: You can permanently delete your account and data directly from the app Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account.
