Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At Palette Pro, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data unless you create an account. When you create an account, we store your email address and encrypted password.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide "Cloud Sync" features, allowing you to access your saved palettes across multiple devices.

3. Data Deletion

You may request full deletion of your account and data at any time via the app settings or by contacting support@palettepro.app.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for payment processing. We explicitly do not use these services for user tracking. These services are compliant with GDPR and CCPA regulations.
